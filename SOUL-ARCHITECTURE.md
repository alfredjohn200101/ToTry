# To Try — The Soul Architecture
*Not a feature list. The intention behind every page, who it serves, and how a man moves through it.*

## The one sentence
To Try is the big brother a man never had — a presence that sees the WHOLE of him (money, mind,
body, spirit) and helps him wake with purpose, stay grounded in himself and in Christ, and keep
walking while the world pulls him toward his passions. Not a tracker. A brother.

## Who we build for
**One man, across his whole life.** He wakes wanting purpose. The day pulls at him from every side —
addictions, easy pleasures, an algorithm built to feed his passions, debt, doubt, exhaustion. He is
often the oldest, the one others lean on, with no one above him to say "I've walked this, here's
what's next, let me slap you back to reality with love." That's who this is. The man who needed a
big brother and never had one — so he becomes the proof that one can exist, even in a phone.

He is not a crisis. He is a whole life in motion. We serve him when he's winning AND when he's about
to fall — because a real brother is there for the whole climb, not just the cliff edge.

## The mechanism that makes it a brother, not a dashboard
Every other app is ONE feature of a man — a macro tracker, a workout log, a budget, a prayer app, a
sobriety counter. Each sees a sliver and is blind to the rest. To Try is the one home where they ALL
live together — and *because* they live together, the data stops being tracking and becomes COUNSEL.
A macro tracker logs the food. The brother knows he trained hard, slept badly, went over his line
last night, and his debt's been weighing on him — so he can say something TRUE that no single-feature
app could ever say. The integration is not a feature. It is the entire point.

Every screen must do more than track. It must answer at least one of:
**what's next for him · what's wrong · what can he do better.** If it only reports, it isn't done.

## The line we will not cross
Feeding passions for engagement is what harms men. This app must be the OPPOSITE — it pulls a man
back to himself and to Christ, even at the cost of its own screen time. It points beyond itself: to
the sacraments, to real relationships, to prayer. It never makes itself the authority (Magnifica
Humanitas — AI as signpost, not god). A brother who keeps you for himself is not a brother. This one
gives you back to your life, better than it found you.

## EVERY PAGE — purpose · who it serves · the feeling · how it connects

### TODAY (home) — *the front door*
- **Purpose:** orient the man the second he opens the app. Tell him where he is in his day and the
  ONE thing that matters now.
- **Serves:** both. The 11pm man (the companion is one reach away) and the winning man (his days-in,
  his becoming, right at the top).
- **Feeling:** "this knows me, this is with me." The aurora breathes with the time of day so the app
  is a companion through the day, not a static board.
- **Connects:** → morning/evening ritual (the action button), → companion (the breathing orb,
  reachable from everywhere), → everything else below the fold, quieter.
- **DONE (v159):** the breathing front-door hero. The rest of the home below it still needs to be
  ordered by *meaning* (what serves the man now), not by accretion.

### THE COMPANION (not a tab — a presence) — *the heart*
- **Purpose:** the 11pm moment. Meet the man in the exact second of temptation.
- **Serves:** the 11pm man. This is the whole reason the app exists.
- **Feeling:** a friend who already knows your day, doesn't flinch, doesn't preach. Reachable from
  ANY screen with one thumb-motion (the breathing orb → should become a swipe-up sheet).
- **Connects:** knows everything (sleep, training, vices, faith) so it can say "the pull is strong

## THE DESIGN LANGUAGE (one soul, applied everywhere)
- **Daypart aurora** — dawn/day/dusk/night skins, so the whole app breathes with time.
- **Three-layer elevation** — base/raised/overlay, consistent depth (DONE, v158).
- **Spring motion that confirms, never performs** — every tap answers "did that land?"
- **Bottom-sheets** for secondary content (the iOS convention thumbs expect).
- **One focus per screen** — earn attention before asking for it; never a wall of cards.
- **Cormorant serif for the soul-voice; DM Mono for the quiet data-voice.** Already the palette.
- **Grace over shame, always. Presence over measurement. The app points beyond itself.**

## THE BUILD ORDER (intention, not vanity)
1. Companion as a bottom-sheet (the heart — most important thing in the app).
2. Morning + Evening rebuilt to FEEL like their time of day (the daily rhythm).
3. The body loop (Train→Nourish→Track) made to flow as one.
4. Fight reframed as "your fight, and how you're winning."
5. Soul as the still center.
6. Money as stewardship.
7. Home below-the-fold ordered by meaning.
8. Calendar: question its existence; fold if it doesn't serve.
9. Cohesion pass: every screen inherits the daypart + elevation + one-focus language.
