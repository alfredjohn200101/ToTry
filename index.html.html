The mission

Raw daily documentation of a real transformation. No performance. No pretence. The story is the product. To Try is the proof.

Day 1 reel — structure
01 — Open mid-sentence (first 3 sec)
"I lost the person I loved most because of who I chose to be." — no intro, no hey guys, start here.
02 — The admission (30–60 sec)
Briefly name your problems. Debt. Addictions. The breakup. No detail needed — just honesty. People feel the truth before they understand it.
03 — The decision (20 sec)
Not motivation. A decision. "I'm not changing what I do. I'm changing who I am."
04 — Show the app (15 sec)
Screen record. Show one thing — the vice tab, or a Bible verse appearing. "I built this because I couldn't afford what others charge. It's free."
05 — The sign-off
"I don't know what I'm doing. But from the moment this goes up — I'm trying. The most I can do, hopefully, is to try."
