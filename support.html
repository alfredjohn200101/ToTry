Common things

How do I sign in?
Enter your email; you get a 6-digit code. No password to remember. Your email is your account.

How do I get reminders?
Settings → Daily reminders → Enable. Choose your morning and evening times. If you want it, turn on the gentle check-in at your hardest hour.

Is it free?
Yes. No ads, no subscription, no paywalled features.

How do I export or delete my data?
Settings → Your data → Export, or Settings → Account → Delete account permanently. Both are instant and yours to do anytime.

A food's numbers look wrong.
Open the food, tap "Numbers look wrong? Correct them," and type the label. Your numbers are saved for every future scan.

If you're in crisis, To Try is not a substitute for real help. Please reach a professional or a helpline in your country. In Australia: Lifeline 13 11 14.
